Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member of the badminton club?

To become a member, please visit our Membership page on our website. You'll find detailed information about the application process, membership fees, and the benefits of joining.

What are the benefits of becoming a club member?

Club members enjoy exclusive access to our badminton courts, participation in coaching programs, discounted rates for events, and a vibrant community of fellow badminton enthusiasts. Check out our "Membership" page for a comprehensive list of benefits.

What are the club's operating hours?

Our regular operating hours are [9:00am to 5:00pm Mon-Fri]. Be sure to check our News updates page for any changes, especially during holidays or special events.

How can I get updates on club news and events?

Stay informed by visiting our website regularly, following us on social media, and subscribing to our newsletter. The latest updates can be found on our News updates page.

Are there social events or tournaments organized by the club?

Yes, we regularly organize social events, tournaments, and friendly competitions. Keep an eye on our Events page for upcoming activities.

Can I bring a guest to play at the club?

Guests are welcome! Please review our guest policy on the Membership page for information on fees and any restrictions.

Are there coaching programs available for beginners?

Absolutely! We offer coaching programs tailored for players of all skill levels.

What equipment do I need to bring for playing at the club?
